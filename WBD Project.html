Hillary Clinton:

Hillary Clinton first rose to elective office in 2001 and stepped down from an elected position in 2009. Clinton has served as an elected official for a total of 8 years, which is 7 less than the average 2016 presidential candidate (15 years).

Campaign Fundraising Summary

Total Raised: $542 Million
Strong Fundraising Numbers: As of August 31, 2016, Hillary Clinton raised a total of $542M across all affiliated campaign committees. Clinton raised the most of all 2016 presidential candidates and 28 times more than the average ($19.7M). Clinton's official committee raised more than any other affiliated committee.

Official Committee: Hillary for America

Super PACs: Priorities USA, Correct the Record, and American Bridge

Donald Trump:

Donald Trump has never held elective office. He has experience in business and management. Donald Trump is the owner of many companies and organizations.

Campaign Fundraising Summary

Strong Fundraising Numbers: As of August 31, 2016, Donald Trump raised a total of $185M across all affiliated campaign committees.
